Title: On the Perception of Difficulty: Differences between Humans and AI
Authors: Philipp Spitzer, Joshua Holstein, Michael Vössing, Niklas Kühl
Summary: With the increased adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) in industry and society, effective human-AI interaction systems are becoming increasingly important. A central challenge in the interaction of humans with AI is the estimation of difficulty for human and AI agent
